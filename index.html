Food Finder

Here on Food Finder you can find popular dishes from different restraunts in different parts of Ames. You can scroll through all the options in an area or you can filter based on the kind of food your looking for(e.g. Past, Burgers, or Tacos. Any item that fits what your looking for in your part of Ames will show up telling you what restraunt its at and how much it costs. Below are some of the more popular restraunt in Ames as well.

Jeffs Pizza is an Ames classic right across from campus

Hickory park is a historic Ames restraunt on Duff avenu

Cornbread is an Ames barbeque restraunt

Macubana is a restraunt serving inventive macaroni dishes located right on welch avenue.

Clydes is a burger restraunt located in the center of campus.

Mr. Burrito is a small restraunt located right across campus on lincoln way.
